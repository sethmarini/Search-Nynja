Welcome to the Career Services Resource Library! The Career Services team is committed to providing career resources that will help you: Successfully navigate your job search Address questions and concerns pertaining to the job search Increase your knowledge base regarding all aspects of job searching in today’s technical market Develop your professional expertise and branding in the technical industry The Career Services Resource Library contains articles, templates and guides focusing on a variety of job search and career and professional development topics. You’ll find resources addressing, among other topics: Resume and Job Search Correspondence Templates Interview Preparation Portfolio & Online Profile Guides Job Search Guidance Career Curriculum Decks The resources have been developed by Career Services professionals, targeting students and alumni of our programs. You’ll find information specifically catered to your experience as a student. Our goal is to provide you with up-to-date, relevant career information. Continue to visit the Career Services Resource Library to help develop your career and job search knowledge. The Career Services team wishes you the best in your pursuit of work in the technical profession. Your Boot Camp experience, combined with the Career Services resources that include this library will hopefully empower you to continue your professional development in this always-changing industry.
